This is why we are the acoustic duo for you!

What's our vibe?

Our goal is to play to suit the needs of the venue while keeping our high energy levels to get all of the crowd interacting with us. Our spanish, flaminco style is suitable for any venue type, whether you need something chilled or fast paced that will draw the crowd in. We mainly cover songs but with our own twist so everyone will recognise what we are playing and due to this, in most cases the crowd will sing along to us.

Event types?

We cater for pretty much any event type, up to date we have played at the following venue/event types;

  • Pubs
  • Cafes
  • Resturants
  • Birthday Parties
  • Festivals
  • Weddings

We are very flexible so please get in touch with us by filling in our Contact Us Form.

Will we take song requests?

Why yes, yes we will! However, song requests will depend on the nature of the event. For pubs, festivals, cafes & resturants we will taylor our style to fit the vibe of the venue but unfortunately we wouldn't learn new songs specifically for such events. Birthdays and weddings, well, we would love to make your big day even more special! We can adpapt most styles of music into our Spanish acoustic vibe so please be sure to ask when enquiring to availability. Let's make the day yours!
